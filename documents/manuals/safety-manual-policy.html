Statement of Safety Policy

Safety Manual Revision {{BRAND_REVISION_NUMBER}}

MH Construction, Inc. recognizes that the safety and health of our employees, subcontractors, and the general public is of paramount importance. We are committed to maintaining a safe and healthful workplace by complying with all applicable federal, state, and local safety regulations and standards.

This Safety Manual outlines our commitment to safety excellence and provides comprehensive guidance for all personnel working on MH Construction projects. Every employee, subcontractor, and visitor is responsible for maintaining a safe work environment and reporting hazardous conditions immediately.

Our safety program is designed to prevent workplace injuries and illnesses through hazard identification, risk assessment, proper training, and continuous improvement. We believe that safety is not just a regulatory requirement, but a core value that reflects our commitment to protecting the well-being of everyone on our projects.

This policy is effective as of {{BRAND_REVISION_DATE}} and applies to all MH Construction operations and personnel.
