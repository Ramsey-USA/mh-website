## 6) Approach for Occupied Inpatient Facility and Client Privacy

MH Construction understands the sensitivity of performing work in an active inpatient treatment environment where privacy and safety are mission-critical.

Our operational approach includes:

- Strict zone control and controlled access between treatment and construction areas
- Physical barriers and pathway separation to protect clients and staff
- Periodic worker orientations and signed privacy acknowledgments for all workers and subcontractors prior to site entry
- Phased planning with ICRA-aligned temporary conditions for cutovers and long-lead sequencing
- Active coordination with facility operations throughout all phases
- Immediate escalation and documented corrective action protocols for site issues
