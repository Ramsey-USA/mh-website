Welcome

Welcome to MH Construction Inc.! The purpose of this employee handbook is to briefly outline the business services provided by MH Construction Inc., provide you with a general understanding of the policies by which MH Construction Inc. operates, and provide an outline of the procedures to be followed by each employee to maintain a smooth running, professional, and profitable operation.

A strong and productive MH Construction Inc. team can be achieved by strong working relationships, based on mutual respect and understanding. A system of established policies and practices provides the foundation for these relationships.

This employee handbook has been prepared to provide you with general information about

the policies, procedures, and benefits of MH Construction Inc.. Contact your supervisor for more detailed information.

Company Culture

MH Construction Inc. employees are expected to contribute to a safe and healthy work environment that promotes cooperative working relationships based on mutual respect and understanding. Because each role is important to the overall success of MH Construction Inc., all employees are asked to complete their assigned task skillfully and efficiently.
