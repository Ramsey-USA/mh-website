Welcome to MH Construction, Inc.

Welcome to the team. This Employee Handbook introduces the services MH Construction, Inc. provides, gives you a general understanding of the policies by which we operate, and outlines the procedures each team member follows to keep our operation smooth, professional, and successful.

A strong, productive MH Construction, Inc. team is built on strong working relationships grounded in mutual respect and understanding. Our established policies and practices provide the foundation for those relationships.

This handbook is prepared to give you general information about the policies, procedures, and benefits of MH Construction, Inc. Contact your supervisor whenever you need more detailed information.

Who We Are

MH Construction, Inc. is a veteran-owned general contractor serving the Tri-Cities and the greater Pacific Northwest. We build lasting value for our Client Partners through disciplined craftsmanship, transparent communication, and a safety-driven culture. Every project we deliver reflects the standard we hold for ourselves: quality work, delivered with integrity, by a team that takes pride in the result.

Company Culture

Every MH Construction, Inc. team member is expected to contribute to a safe and healthy work environment that promotes cooperative working relationships based on mutual respect and understanding. Because each role matters to the overall success of the company, we ask every team member to complete their assigned work skillfully and efficiently.

We win as a team. When we communicate openly, hold one another accountable, and put safety and quality first, we deliver the experience our Client Partners expect and earn the trust that keeps our business strong.

How to Use This Handbook

This handbook is organized into chapters that group related policies together, making it easy to find the information you need. Several chapters include an acknowledgment form that confirms you have received and understood the policies in that area. Please read each chapter carefully, keep this handbook for reference, and reach out to your supervisor or the Human Resources Department with any questions.

Information Posting

MH Construction, Inc. maintains a bulletin board where important notices are posted. It is your responsibility to read the bulletin board on a regular basis and stay aware of posted items, including required labor-law notices and company announcements.
