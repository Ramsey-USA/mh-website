Acceptable Use of Company Technology

Company computers, mobile devices, software, e-mail, and network access are provided to team members for MH Construction, Inc. business. Securing company and Client Partner data is a responsibility every team member shares. Team members must protect proprietary information through regular password maintenance, appropriate access controls, and by following any information security procedures the company puts in place. Related agreements (Computer and Electronics Use Agreement) accompany this chapter.

E-mail, Phone, and Mail

The use of a company email address is for company business only; do not provide your company email for personal communications or personal online accounts. Management reserves the right to monitor e-mail and to have all passwords and access codes for files created on company time or for the company's benefit. If any unauthorized team member accesses another team member's files, company records, e-mail, or voice mail without specific authorization, they may be subject to a warning or termination depending on the severity of the access.

Limit the number of personal calls made or taken during business hours, and keep conversations under three minutes so as not to tie up the business lines. MH Construction, Inc. does not release personal information to outside callers. Long-distance personal calls should not be made on business lines; obtain your supervisor's approval if you need to make one, and you will be expected to reimburse the company for the call. All personal mail must be directed to your home, and personal business activities are not to be conducted during working hours.

Company Vehicle Policies and Procedures

A related acknowledgment form (Company Vehicle Policies and Procedures Acknowledgement) accompanies this section. The following requirements apply to all assigned company vehicles:

  • Eligibility to drive a company vehicle. Team members eligible to drive a company vehicle are selected at the discretion of the company. Prior to vehicle assignment, eligible team members must prove they hold a valid driver's license that is not suspended or revoked. MH Construction, Inc. will also review your driving record and periodically run a Motor Vehicle Record (MVR), as required by our insurance carrier, during your assignment.
  • Driver responsibilities. If a team member's driver's license is revoked, suspended, or restricted for any reason, it is mandatory that an MH Construction, Inc. manager be notified immediately.
  • Scope of use. No person other than the assigned team member shall operate the vehicle unless that person is a team member of MH Construction, Inc., is on the approved driver list, and has permission from the assigned team member or a supervisor. Possession, transportation, or consumption of alcohol or drugs by anyone in the vehicle is not allowed. Drivers and all passengers must wear available personal restraints. Report any accident immediately to police and MH Construction, Inc. management. Company-owned vehicles are for company business only; personal use is prohibited, and personal trailers, boats, and recreational vehicles are not to be pulled. Company vehicles may be driven home and used for transportation to and from work only if approved by management. All company tools, equipment, and electronics must be reasonably stored and secured; loss or theft of company property due to negligence is at the sole expense of the team member, and loss or theft of personal belongings from a company vehicle is the team member's sole responsibility. No pets are allowed in company vehicles.
  • Maintenance and upkeep. Drivers are responsible for ensuring the vehicle is well maintained, including maintaining the vehicle's maintenance and fuel log and coordinating scheduled service with the Warehouse Manager or General Manager. Turn in all repair receipts and maintenance records for filing. Report any damage, burned-out bulbs, and fuses for lights, turn signals, headlights, and horn immediately. Keep the vehicle clean and orderly—at a minimum, the interior washed monthly and the exterior washed bi-weekly. Random inspections will be conducted by management to ensure safe serviceability, and logs are to be furnished upon request. Failure to maintain a clean and orderly vehicle will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
  • Return of company property. Team members are required to return all company property upon separation of employment. Final paychecks may be withheld only as permitted by applicable law until all company property has been returned. Where allowed by law and with any required written authorization, the company may deduct or recover the value of unreturned or damaged property from a team member's final wages.

Temporary Remote-Work Policy

Telecommuting—also called teleworking, working from home, or working remotely—is a work arrangement in which a team member works outside of the office. Related forms (Temporary Work From Home Application/Agreement and Computer and Electronics Use Agreement) accompany this section. Candidates for remote-work arrangements must be trustworthy, disciplined, and self-motivated, and must possess good time-management and organizational skills. A team member may be eligible to temporarily work remotely if their duties can be met through basic hardware and software; not all jobs can be performed off-site, and positions requiring face-to-face interaction with Client Partners and office personnel typically do not meet remote-work requirements.

Team members working remotely must carry out the same duties and responsibilities they do on MH Construction, Inc. premises, remain available for scheduled meetings and required office activities, and agree with their manager on the work schedule and the manner and frequency of communication. Securing data and company information is of the utmost concern: team members are expected to protect proprietary company and Client Partner information through locked file cabinets and desks, regular password maintenance, and other appropriate measures. Any breach in security protocol will lead to strict and swift disciplinary action. MH Construction, Inc. will provide office supplies and furnishings such as a computer with applicable software, keyboard, mouse, and monitors; company equipment is to be used for business purposes only and returned upon termination. All company policies regarding conduct, electronics use, confidentiality, and more continue to apply regardless of physical work location. MH Construction, Inc. has the right to cancel or suspend remote-work privileges at any time and will make every effort to provide 30 days' notice.
