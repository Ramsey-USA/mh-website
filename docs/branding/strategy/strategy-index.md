## üìã Overview

Strategic brand documentation covering company identity, messaging framework, voice and tone, and content standards
WITH military-construction terminology integration (December 2025 major update).

These documents define WHO MH Construction is (veteran-owned, all-branch military values) and HOW we communicate
our brand (military operations terminology blended with construction expertise).

**Strategic Focus (December 2025):** Build an award-winning website that attracts professional veterans through:

- Authentic service-earned messaging and tactical terminology
- All-branch military recognition (Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, Coast Guard, Space Force)
- Veteran-friendly career pathways and growth opportunities
- Military precision meets construction excellence positioning
- THE ROI IS THE RELATIONSHIP partnership philosophy

**Military/Construction Methodology:**

- Honors all service branches: Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, Coast Guard, Space Force
- Core mission central: "Building projects for the client, NOT the dollar"
- Service-earned values: SITREP-level honesty, mission-first integrity, military bearing professionalism
- Tactical terminology: Mission brief, recon, deployment, rally point, operations
- Professional veteran recruitment focus

### Military Terminology Standards

- **Mission Brief**: Initial consultation/project discussion
- **SITREP**: Situation report / project update
- **Recon**: Site visit / preliminary assessment
- **Rally Point**: Contact/consultation booking
- **Deployment**: Project execution phase
- **All-Branch Recognition**: Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, Coast Guard, Space Force

### Brand Colors

- **Hunter Green**: `#386851` - Primary brand color (professionalism, growth, military precision)
- **Leather Tan**: `#BD9264` - Secondary accent (warmth, craftsmanship, service)

### Target Audience

- **Primary**: Homeowners (residential projects) + veteran-affiliated clients
- **Secondary**: Business owners (light commercial) + government contracts
- **Tertiary**: Property managers, developers, all-branch military community
