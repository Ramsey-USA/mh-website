# Review Outreach Templates

**Category:** Marketing - Templates
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

> **Brand Congruency:** Keep language direct, relationship-first, and neutral. Request honest feedback only.

## Usage Notes

- Use these templates only after a real closeout milestone.
- Do not ask for five-star ratings.
- Do not ask for specific phrasing.
- Keep one follow-up maximum unless stakeholder re-engages.

## Email Template - Initial Request (Day 3 to Day 7)

Subject options:

- Thank you for partnering with MH Construction
- Quick favor: share your project experience
- Your feedback helps future project stakeholders
