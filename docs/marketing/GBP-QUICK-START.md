1. **Access Your Google Business Profile**
   - Go to: <https://business.google.com/dashboard>
   - Verify you're logged in with correct account

2. **Complete Your Profile (30 minutes, one-time)**
   - Verify all business info is current
   - Add business description (use template from guide)
   - List all services offered
   - Add business attributes (veteran-owned, etc.)
   - Upload 10-20 existing photos

3. **Create Your First Post (5 minutes)**
   - Use a template from `GBP-POST-TEMPLATES.md`
   - Customize with current info
   - Add a photo
   - Post on Monday morning

4. **Set Up Notifications**
   - Enable email notifications for new reviews
   - Download Google Business app on your phone
   - Set reminder for weekly tasks
