## Veterans Annual Fishing Event

### Event Vision

Starting **Spring 2026**, MH Construction will host an **Annual Veterans Fishing Benefit Event** to
honor our service members, connect the veteran community, and provide therapeutic outdoor
experiences for those who've served.

### Event Details

#### Scale & Participation

- **40+ Boats** from the local boating community
- **Target Participation**: 100-150 veterans and their families
- **Location**: Pacific Northwest waters (specific location TBD)
- **Duration**: Full-day fishing event with morning departure and evening return
- **Timeline**: Annual event every spring (target: April-May)

#### Event Components

**Morning:**

- 6:00 AM registration and breakfast
- 6:30 AM safety briefing and boat assignments
- 7:00 AM departure from marina

**Day Activities:**

- Guided fishing with experienced local captains
- Professional fishing instruction for beginners
- Camaraderie and veteran networking on the water
- Photography and storytelling opportunities

**Evening:**

- 5:00 PM return to dock
- Fish cleaning and preparation
- 6:00 PM BBQ dinner and awards ceremony
- Recognition of top catches and participants
- Live music and veteran speakers

#### Event Goals

1. **Therapeutic Experience**: Provide peaceful outdoor recreation promoting mental health
2. **Community Building**: Connect veterans across service branches and generations
3. **Family Support**: Include veteran families in healing and bonding experiences
4. **Public Awareness**: Raise awareness of veteran needs and contributions
5. **Long-term Impact**: Create annual tradition veterans can look forward to

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Veterans

**Q: Is there a cost to participate?**  
A: No. The event is fully sponsored by our community partners at no cost to veterans.

**Q: Can I bring family members?**  
A: Yes! Veterans may bring 1-2 family members (space permitting).

**Q: Do I need fishing experience?**  
A: No experience necessary. Experienced captains and volunteers will provide instruction.

**Q: What if I can't swim?**  
A: Life jackets provided for all participants. Safety is our top priority.

### For Sponsors

**Q: Are sponsorships tax-deductible?**  
A: Yes, sponsorships qualify as charitable contributions (consult your tax advisor).

**Q: Can we send company representatives?**  
A: Absolutely! Sponsor tiers include complimentary registrations.

**Q: How is sponsor recognition provided?**  
A: Recognition varies by tier‚Äîsee sponsorship details above.

### For Boat Captains

**Q: What are the requirements?**  
A: Valid Coast Guard safety equipment, licensed captain, liability insurance (we provide coverage).

**Q: Will I be reimbursed for fuel?**  
A: Yes, captains receive fuel reimbursement and complimentary meals.

**Q: How many passengers per boat?**  
A: Typically 3-4 veterans per boat (based on vessel capacity).
