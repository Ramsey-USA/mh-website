### **4. User Journey Tracking** 🛤️

#### Question: "What path do users take before contacting us?"

**Journey Milestones:**

1. `entered_site` - Landing page
2. `viewed_services` - Services page visit
3. `viewed_projects` - Projects page visit
4. `viewed_contact` - Contact page visit
5. `started_form` - Form interaction begins
6. `completed_form` - Successful conversion

**Marketing Insights:**

```text
✓ "Most common path to conversion" → Sequential analysis
✓ "How many pages before contact?" → Journey length
✓ "Services → Contact direct conversion" → Skip rate
✓ "Time to conversion" → Average decision time
```

**Conversion Funnel:**

```text
100 Visitors Enter Site
↓
75 View Services (75% engagement)
↓
45 View Projects (45% engagement)
↓
30 View Contact (30% intent)
↓
15 Start Form (15% serious interest)
↓
10 Complete Form (10% conversion rate)
```

**Access:** Dashboard → "Conversion Funnel Analysis"
